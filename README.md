### The Problem: The "Where Was I?" Tax
We’ve all been there. 

You spend weeks carefully planning the ultimate layout for your *Stardew Valley* farm, mapping out a complex layer hierarchy in *Photoshop*, or deep in the zone refactoring a database inside *VS Code*. Then, life happens. You take a break for a few days, a week, or a month. 

When you finally return and boot up the application, you’re hit with a wall of mental fog:
* 🌾 **In Stardew Valley:** *“What was I doing in the mines again? Which villager was I supposed to gift today?”*
* 🎨 **In Photoshop:** *“Which assets was I supposed to export next, and what feedback did the client give me on this layer?”*
* 💻 **In VS Code:** *“Why did I leave this specific file open? What bug was I trying to patch before I closed this workspace?”*

Internal quest logs don't save your personal master plans, and opening a separate, generic notepad app completely breaks your flow. You waste the first 20 minutes of your session just trying to re-learn your own progress.

---

### The Solution: A Process-Aware Quest Log
Owlnest solves this by acting as an external brain that links your thoughts directly to your tools. It operates like a tactical, cozy owl scout:

1. **The Hunting Grounds (Tracking):** You tell Owlnest which applications, IDEs, or games you want it to keep an eye on.
2. **Spotted! (Auto-Detection):** The application monitors active desktop processes. The second you switch to a tracked window, it locks onto the target.
3. **The Scout Report (Instant Context):** A compact window surfaces immediately, giving you a quick brief:
   * **📍 Last Tracked Spot:** Where you dropped your pack last time.
   * **🎯 The Trail Ahead:** Your exact step-by-step game plan or coding logic for this session.

No backtracking, no forgotten projects, and no wasted momentum. Owlnest remembers the plan so you don't have to, letting you dive straight back into the action—whether you're harvesting crops, designing graphics, or shipping code.

## ✨ Features
*   **Application Tracking**: Automatically remembers which applications you were using.
*   **Smart Recall**: Quickly jump back to previous work progress with search and history.
*   **Seamless Note-taking**: Capture progress instantly with our built-in capture modal that triggers when application session ends.
*   **Privacy First**: Built as a native desktop application to keep your productivity data where it belongs—on your machine.

## 🚀 Getting Started
1.  Go to the [Latest Releases](https://github.com/ascxiao/owlnest-releases/releases/latest) page.
2.  Download the `Owlnest_x.x.x_x64_en-US.msi` (for Windows) or the Setup EXE.
3.  Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
4.  Launch Owlnest from your Start Menu.
